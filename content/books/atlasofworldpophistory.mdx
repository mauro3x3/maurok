I love this book so much. I am obsessed with numbers and how they help explain the world around us and this book is really a great reminder of a lot of historical events i.e. The fall of Rome can really be seen when you look at population growth across Europe between 200 AD and 1300 AD.

Iberia, Italy, and the Balkans barely grew, while Northern Europe saw its population double, triple, or even quadruple.
